Information we receive and how it is used

Information we receive about you:

We receive a number of different types of information about you, including:

(1)Your information

Your information is the information that's required when you sign up for the site.

(2)Registration information:

When you sign up for MyTab, you are required to provide information such as your name, email address and password.

(3)Third Party Accounts information:

For you to start integrating accounts into MyTab, we ask for the username and password of those accounts. All the credentials supplied by you undergo thorough encryption before storing them in our database. We DO NOT, at any point in time, have plaintext access to those credentials. In other words, we can never see those credentials

How we use the information we receive :

We use the information we receive about you and your accounts in connection with the services and features we provide to you.We store data for as long as it is necessary to provide products and services to you and others, including those described above. Typically, information associated with your account will be kept until your account is deleted. For certain categories of data, we may also tell you about specific data retention practices.
