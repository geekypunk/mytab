How cookies work

What are cookies?

Cookies are small files that are placed on your browser or device by the website. Like most websites, we use cookies to provide you with a better, faster or safer experience.

How and why are cookies used?

  • Security and site integrity: We use these to help keep MyTab safe and secure. They support or enable security features and help us detect activity that violates our terms.For example, they help protect your account from being accessed by anyone other than you. Cookies also let us know when several people have logged in from the same computer.
  • Authentication : These tools tell us when you are logged in, so we can show you the appropriate experience and features. They help us know who you are so we can show content that is relevant to you.
  • Performance: We use these to provide you with the best experience possible.
  • Site features and services: These provide functionality that help us deliver products and services.
  • Research, understand the use of, and improve our products and services

When might MyTab place cookies on my computer or device?

MyTab may place cookies when you visit us or a partner using a browser or device that permits the placement of cookies. Your browser or device may allow you to block these technologies, but you may not be able to use some features on MyTab if you block them. Note that your browser or device may or may not include_once tools that allow you to manage cookies. For more information about whether tools are available, how they work, and what they do, visit your browser or device's help materials. Generally, tools like these in your browser or device affect only that particular browser or device. So if you are using multiple browsers or devices, you can make different choices for each of them.
